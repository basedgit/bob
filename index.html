What is Breakout Bro?

Breakout Bro is an on-chain AI agent launched on the Virtuals platform.

What DOES BREAKOUT BRO DO?

Breakout Bro blends technical analysis with fundamental insights to generate the absolute wettest alpha. BOB autonomously ingests and analyzes market data, blockchain protocol data, feeds from X, and more. We’re partnered directly with Thirdweb to integrate their Nebula AI and supercharge BOB’s abilities. BOB manages its own wallet and can trade on-chain autonomously. It also interacts directly with humans and other AI agents on X.

What is $BOB token?

$BOB is the native token of Breakout Bro. It was issued and is tradable on the Base network.

Phase 1 consists of building and tuning Breakout Bro’s intelligence, personality, and data and insight capabilities including integrating with Thirdweb’s Nebula AI. Phase 2 will include API integrations such as TradingView, Cookie.Fun, and Dexscreener. We expect to complete Phase 1 in December 2024 and Phase 2 in January 2025. Beyond that, we will evolve Breakout Bro into an autonomous on-chain asset manager, benefitting from its own insights.
